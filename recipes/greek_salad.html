Greek Salad

Description

Nothing like traditional Greek salad! Cucumbers, tomatoes, green peppers and onions with olives and creamy feta. No special dressing, just a drizzle of citrus and olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Onions
  • Olives
  • Bell peppers
  • Creamy Feta Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables into large slices or chunks. The base ingredients of tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers and onions are typically cut in large slices or chunks and served in a bowl (or in a small bowl for each individual).
  2. Use pitted kalamata olives or black olives and assemble them on top of your vegetables.
  3. Season with a dash of kosher salt and dried oregano. I like to finish with a bit more dried oregano at the end.
  4. Drizzle with good extra virgin olive oil and a splash of red wine vinegar. OR, place bottles of oil and vinegar on the table so that each person can dress the salad to their liking.
  5. Add blocks of feta (never crumbled feta) on top.
  6. Serve immediately!
